How to format your links

Since sites use hidden ID numbers for exact episodes, use their Search URLs as your template. Replace the movie name in the search bar with {title}.

Templates for your sites:

MyFlixerz / HuraWatch

https://kisskh.co/Search?q={title}
HiAnime
https://hianime.to/search?keyword={title}

https://turkish123.com/?s={title}
